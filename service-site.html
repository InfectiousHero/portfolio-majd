About Us

PrestigeCuts has been on Sparks Street since 2018. We're three barbers who care about what we do — Marcus, Darius, and James. No huge chain, no assembly line, just us and our regulars.

We do a quick consultation before every cut. You know what you want? Great. Not sure? We'll figure it out together. Either way, you're leaving with something you actually like.
